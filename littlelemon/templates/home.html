Our Story

A family recipe, perfected over generations

Founded in 2026, Little Lemon began with a simple dream: to bring the vibrant, sun-kissed flavors of the Mediterranean to your table. Every dish tells a story of tradition, crafted with locally-sourced ingredients and a passion for authenticity.

From our hand-rolled pasta to our signature lemon-infused desserts, we believe that great food is about more than taste—it's about the memories you create around the table.
